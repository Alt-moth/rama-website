RE-NUP

Current Project: Spousal Reunification and Integration Laws in Europe

RE-NUP tracks the lives of South Asian immigrants in Germany, Italy, and France. In particular, I am interested in the experiences of married couples and families who need to apply for spousal/family visas and attend language and cultural classes as part of the integration process.

If you are interested in finding out more click here. If you would like to join the project as a research participant please get in touch at rama.srinivasan@unive.it.
